Change Talkingheads
Use this: parent.ResetTalkingHeads(c1,p1,s1,c2,p2,s2,c3,p3,s3,c4,p4,s4), ci is the name of the character and pi is the location of the character file. si is for showing or not. Example: 

parent.ResetTalkingHeads(
'Ben','CBCode/English/Paul/Output/Ben_Files','true',
'Anna','CBCode/English/Julie/Output/Anna_Files','true',
'Jason','CBCode/English/Paul/Output/Jason_Files','false',
'Carl','CBCode/English/Paul/Output/Carl_Files','false',)
